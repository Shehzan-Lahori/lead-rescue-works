Workflow path.

The first paid job should stay narrow: one source, one record, one alert, one fix.

CaptureReceive a lead from a form, ad, webhook, email, or GHL workflow.
NormalizeMap names, phone, email, service, source, message, and next action.
AlertNotify the owner or sales channel with the fields needed to follow up.
TrackWrite the lead to Sheet, Airtable, CRM, or a lightweight audit log.
DebugFor broken workflows, isolate whether the problem is trigger, payload, mapping, auth, or downstream handling.
